BIOGRAPHY

I am currently 14, a 9th grader attending MAS Zelda Glazer 6-12 in Miami, FL. I love studying history and art. I plan to major in art and become an animator. I see myself animating cartoons, drawing, or writing in the future. I want to learn figure drawing and animation. Ever since I was a kid, I was watching cartoons or playing video games. I have always wanted to animate ever since I saw my first cartoon. My love for technology began when I was a small child. I was 5 and my parents gave me my first computer. My mom and sister showed me how to use the computer; my sister also taught me how to play games on it. I was introduced by my older sister to video games, she used to play with me online and I really enjoyed going on. Every day I made the effort to play around on my computer. I learned many things and I liked doing what I did. My experiences led me to today, a student at MAS 6-12 and I am glad to be here.

Animator

Animation appeals to me the most because when I was little I loved cartoons. Cartoons brought me joy when I most needed it, and I want to share that emotion with others. By becoming an animator I can achieve my dreams of using my work to bring entertainment.

Storyboard Artist

Secondary option, if being an animator doesn't cut out. Storyboard artists draw the panels for shows, I want to use my talent of drawing to create works to direct the show.

Graphic Designer

I want to study all types of art, so in the future I can keep my sense of art in my career. I see myself painting digitally and selling my art work.

Travel

In the future I would like to travel the world and visit famous places. I am passionate about studying history and want to learn more by traveling.
